FAQ

What time should I arrive?

Please be seated by 1:00 PM — ceremony begins promptly at 2:00 PM.

Is there a dress code?

Yes — kindly follow the dress guide. We especially appreciate modest outfits. Please avoid white.

Where should I park?

Casa de Canitoan has its own parking lot, free for our guests.

Indoors or outdoors?

Garden ceremony followed by an indoor reception at the same venue.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

Yes! You may take photos. Our photographer will also share the official ones with us afterward.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Per pax only — kindly, no additional plus-ones. Thank you for understanding.
