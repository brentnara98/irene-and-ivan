Helpful Reminders

Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is the wedding?
Saturday, June 27, 2026 at 1:00 PM. The ceremony and reception will both be held at Casa de Canitoan, Canitoan, Cagayan de Oro City. We recommend arriving 30 minutes early to be seated comfortably before the prelude.
What is the dress code?
For our principal sponsors — traditional Filipiniana and Barong Tagalog. For all other guests — Sunday's Best in shades of pink and blue (see palette above). Kindly avoid wearing white. Comfortable shoes are recommended as the ceremony is held in a garden setting.
How do I RSVP?
Please confirm your attendance through the RSVP form below on or before June 6, 2026. We have reserved specific seats per invitation, so please honor the names on your invite to help us plan accordingly. You may also reply via Facebook Messenger if preferred.
Can I bring a plus-one?
Only the names on the invitations, please. We appreciate your understanding as we keep our celebration intimate.
Is there parking at the venue?
Yes — please park your vehicles in the designated area outside the venue. Look for signage upon arrival, and our coordinators will assist in directing you.
What about gifts and registry?
Your presence and prayers are already the most precious gift to us. If you'd like to bless us further, we've shared three options above — A Blessing of Choice, our Honeymoon Fund, and A Cozy Home — all accepting digital payments via PayMongo.
Will there be a photographer? Can I take photos?
Yes, we have a professional photography and videography team capturing the day. During the ceremony, we kindly ask that you keep phones and cameras tucked away so our photographers can capture the moment without obstruction. Snap away during the reception — and please share!
Will the ceremony be held outdoors? What if it rains?
Yes, our ceremony is a garden wedding at Casa de Canitoan. The venue has covered options should weather not cooperate. Bringing a small hand fan is a thoughtful idea for warm afternoons.
What time should I arrive?
Please arrive by 12:30 PM so you can be seated before the prelude begins. The ceremony will start promptly at 1:00 PM.
Where can I see more photos after the wedding?
We'll be uploading photos to our shared Google Photos album. Feel free to add any photos you take that day — we'd love to see the day through your eyes too.
Who do I contact for questions?
For any questions or concerns, please message us directly through Facebook Messenger, or speak with any member of our entourage. We're so excited to share this day with you!
